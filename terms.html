Order Regulations

Review and comply with all terms before placing an order.

Nocturne Designs – Terms of Service

Failure to adhere to these rules may result in being blacklisted from ordering services.

💰 Payments & Assets

Clients must pay either 50/50 or fully upfront depending on the designer’s requirements. Final products are delivered unwatermarked only after full payment. PSD/PDN files are not provided.

📜 Details

You are required to provide all necessary information crucial to the designer’s preference for your order. Missing details may delay production.

🚫 Designer Pinging

Consistently pinging a designer to rush your order will result in immediate ticket closure. Refunds will not be issued in such cases.

🎨 Requesting Designers

You may request a specific designer, but arrangements must be made before the order begins.

🔗 Reselling

Reselling our products is only permitted if the designer explicitly allows it. In such cases, proper credit must be given to both the designer and Nocturne Designs.

💳 Refunds

Refunds are not offered after payment unless an issue or mistake is caused by our end. Credits may be issued if approved by HR.

📦 Lost Products

We are not responsible for lost products. Once delivered, clients must store their assets safely.

✏️ Changing Orders

No changes are allowed after completion. Minor adjustments may be requested during development only.

🎟️ Gamepass Purchases

Only purchase gamepasses from individuals with the @Payment Helper role. We are not responsible for scams outside the official Nocturne Designs Purchase Hub.
